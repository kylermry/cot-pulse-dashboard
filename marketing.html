Frequently Asked Questions

What is COT data and why does it matter?

The Commitment of Traders (COT) report is released weekly by the CFTC and shows the positioning of different trader categories in futures markets. It reveals what commercial hedgers (often considered "smart money"), large speculators, and small traders are doing. This data can help you anticipate major market turns.

How often is the data updated?

The CFTC releases new COT data every Friday afternoon (for positions held as of Tuesday). We update our charts and analysis within hours of the official release, so you always have access to the latest positioning data.
